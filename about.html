History

Coffee Hour was established on the year 2017 at Tetuan, Zamboanga City. They established the coffee shop because they found out that there were limited number of coffee shops here in Zamboanga City. Reservation and ordering services are the main transactions of the business of the client. They accommodate reservation for different events especially for meetings. For ordering service, they have walk-in and dine-in services for orders, and a cash-on-delivery service will be added on their services with the used of our project. To increase the number of customers, to increase their income, to escalate the number of people who knows their physical store, to increase their credibility, and provide new way of transaction for their service.
